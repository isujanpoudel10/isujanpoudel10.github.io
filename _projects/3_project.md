**Timeline:** January 2025 - December 2025

This project reflects my work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. It focused on near-field electromagnetic scattering and propagation behavior for THz wireless communication systems.

**Highlights**

- Conducted near-field electromagnetic scattering analysis for THz wireless communication using COMSOL Multiphysics and MATLAB.
- Developed and evaluated propagation models for electromagnetic waves in the near-field region.
- Performed simulation-based data collection, post-processing, and analysis to study wave behavior and communication performance.
- Investigated communication system characteristics relevant to emerging high-frequency wireless systems.

**Focus Areas**

- THz communications
- Near-field propagation
- Electromagnetic modeling
- COMSOL and MATLAB simulation
